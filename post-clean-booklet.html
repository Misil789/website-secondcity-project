Keep It Clean

Quick Maintenance Tips

🧹

Daily: Wipe kitchen counters after cooking — it takes 30 seconds and prevents buildup.

🛁

After showers: Squeegee or wipe down glass to prevent soap scum and hard water spots.

🗑️

Weekly: Empty all trash cans before odors develop. Change bed sheets every 7–10 days.

🌬️

Air quality: Open windows for 10 min daily. Replace HVAC filters every 90 days.
