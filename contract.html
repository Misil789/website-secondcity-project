Terms & Conditions

1. Minimum Commitment Period
This agreement requires a minimum commitment of 3 consecutive months from the service start date listed above. Discounted pricing is contingent on completing this minimum term. If the client cancels or fails to schedule within the agreed frequency before the 3-month minimum is fulfilled, the standard one-time rate will be retroactively applied to all completed visits and the difference will be invoiced.

2. Discount Terms
The recurring discount applies to the standard base rate for the selected service type and home size at the time of booking. Discounts are not stackable with other promotions (e.g., senior discount, referral credit) unless explicitly approved in writing by Second City Scrubbers.

3. Scheduling
The client agrees to maintain the selected cleaning frequency throughout the commitment period. Second City Scrubbers will work with the client to schedule a consistent recurring appointment. Missed or skipped visits (without proper notice — see Section 4) count toward the minimum commitment and do not receive a discount credit.

4. Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

  • Rescheduling: Individual appointments may be rescheduled with at least 48 hours' notice at no charge. Rescheduling with less than 48 hours' notice may incur a $40 late-reschedule fee.
  • Cancellation within commitment period: If the client terminates this agreement before the 3-month minimum, standard (non-discounted) rates apply to all past visits. Second City Scrubbers will issue an adjusted invoice for the difference.
  • Cancellation after commitment period: Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days' written notice (text or email is acceptable). No penalty applies after the minimum term is fulfilled.

5. Service Pause Policy
Clients may pause their recurring schedule once per agreement term for up to 4 weeks, provided at least 7 days' written notice is given. The pause period does not count toward the 3-month minimum, which will be extended accordingly. More than one pause within a term may result in forfeiture of the discounted rate at Second City Scrubbers' discretion.

6. Payment
Payment is due within 3 business days of each completed cleaning visit. Accepted methods: cash, Venmo, Zelle, or check. Accounts overdue by more than 14 days may result in suspension of service and loss of discounted pricing.

7. Access & Home Conditions
The client agrees to provide access to the property at the scheduled time. If a cleaner arrives and cannot gain entry (no-call lockout), a $50 lockout fee applies. Second City Scrubbers reserves the right to decline or modify service if home conditions present a safety concern.

Important: The recurring discount is a loyalty benefit — not a contractual guarantee in future pricing periods. Second City Scrubbers reserves the right to adjust base rates with 30 days' notice. Your discount percentage will remain the same; it will be applied to the updated base rate.

8. Agreement Renewal
After the minimum commitment period, this agreement automatically continues on a month-to-month basis at the same discounted rate until either party provides written notice of cancellation (see Section 4).
